Our Pet-Friendly Philosophy

Pets Are Family Too

At Basil & Sage Homes, we understand that pets are cherished members of your family. That's why we've made most of our properties pet-friendly, allowing you to find a home where both you and your furry companions can thrive.

We believe that responsible pet owners make excellent residents, and we've designed our pet policies to be fair, reasonable, and clear. Our goal is to create a welcoming environment for all residents, including those with pets.

From dogs and cats to small caged animals, we welcome a variety of pets in our communities, with guidelines designed to ensure the comfort and safety of all residents.
